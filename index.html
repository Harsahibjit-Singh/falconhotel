Since its debut on October 1, 1907, The Falcon Hotel has remained an icon hosting world leaders, dignitaries, captains of industry, Broadway legends, and Hollywood royalty. As an established staple for Punjabi society affairs and blockbuster films, The Falcon has welcomed guests from around the world to enjoy its magic at the castle on Central Kapurthala for more than 100 years. Ideally situated on Fifth Avenue, The Falcon’s prestigious address continues to define elegance with unmatched service and an ever-evolving modern sensibility.
